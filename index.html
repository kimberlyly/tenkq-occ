Welcome to our community!

Here’s a fun fact for you. Did you know that before UCSD had the Outback Climbing Center, it was originally two racquetball courts? Back in 1995, we hatched the greatest idea ever to transform them into the coolest indoor climbing and bouldering facility on campus! The Outback Climbing Center -- don’t worry, you can call us the OCC for short -- welcomes everyone from all different backgrounds and climbing experiences. Whether you’re a newbie itching to get out of your comfort zone or a seasoned climber who’s been at it for years, there’s a place for everyone here at our gym. The OCC community is run by students, for students. Our friendly staff is always available to help make your climbing experience here an enjoyable one you’ll never forget.


So, what are you waiting for? Join our OCC family! Fill out the waiver below to start your adventure!
