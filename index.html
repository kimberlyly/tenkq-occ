Welcome to our community!

In 1995 we came up with the great idea to convert two racquetball courts into the coolest climbing and bouldering facility on campus. The Outback Climbing Center is a professionally designed and built complex, well equipped for both the first-timer and the seasoned climber. Our staff are friendly and accessible for belaying, instruction, coaching and advice. Come check it out!
