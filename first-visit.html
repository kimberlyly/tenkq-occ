First Visit

Free first time every quarter!


We want you to get hooked on climbing! As a student of UCSD, each quarter you get a free day pass and rental gear for anyone who hasn't gone to the OCC during the academic quarter. Stop by during any open hours to give it a try!


Step One: Complete Online Waiver

Step Two: Visit the OCC for FREE during open hours

Step Three: Climb, get hooked and buy a membership!
