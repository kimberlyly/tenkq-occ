Location

Directions

We are located just inside the main door of the Canyonview Aquatics Center on the UCSD campus off of Voigt Drive.

  1. From Interstate 5, exit Genesee Avenue and head east
  2. Turn right onto Campus Point Drive
  3. Turn right onto Voigt Drive
  4. We are directly across Warren Fields, in the Canyonview Pool Complex
  5. The closest parking lot is P510. Be sure to follow all posted parking guidelines. Parking is currently free on weekends.
