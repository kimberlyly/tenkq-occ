Directions


The OCC is located just inside the main door of the Canyonview Center directly across Warren Field.

Click here for an interactive map of the campus, or use Google Maps to get directions from your location.

Driving

  1. From Interstate 5, exit onto Genesee Avenue and head east.
  2. Turn right onto Campus Point Drive.
  3. Turn right onto Voigt Drive.
  4. Continue straight across the Interstate 5 overpass.
  5. Continue past the stop sign on the intersection of Voigt and Gilman Drive.
  6. Our facility will be on your right.

Parking

The closest parking lot is P510, which is located to the right of the Canyon Pool Complex. Be sure to follow all posted UCSD parking guidelines. However, parking - including A, B, S, and V spots - are free on the weekends and university holidays!
