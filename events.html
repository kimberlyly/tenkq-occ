Quarterly Events


Every school year, the Outback Climbing Center puts together fun and competitive events that are open to everyone! It doesn’t matter whether you’re a UCSD student or a non-affiliated one, a newcomer or an experienced climber -- anyone and everyone is free to attend. Our OCC community holds annual events once every quarter. We would love to see you there!
